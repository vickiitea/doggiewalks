Overnights


DoggieWalks offers boarding services both in our homes or in yours, whatever you and your pup are most comfortable with. If we stay in your home, we always make sure to treat it as if it were our own. Boarding services include a minimum of three walks per day, all feeding and medication administration needs, and lots of cuddle time and attention. We are able to take care of the pick-up and drop-off of your dog as well!

  • 1 Dog: $85 6pm-11am
  • 2 Dogs: $120 6pm-11am
  • Puppy overnights: $95 6pm-11am
  • 2 dogs: $45 for 30 min 4-5 times a week
