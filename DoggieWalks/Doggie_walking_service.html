Doggie Walking


We offer solo walks and group walks to cater to your dog's needs. Solo walks last 30 minutes or more and take place around your neighborhood. For group walks, our walkers will pick up your pup, place them in a group of 3-7 dogs, and take them to a nearby park to socialize and get tons of exercise. Group walks tend to last anywhere from 1-3 hours. Examples of places we take your dogs are Ford Funston, Baker Beach, Bernal Heights Dog Park, Golden Gate Park, Douglass Park, Dolores Park and more!

  • $40 for 30 min, less than 4 times per week
