About


DoggieWalks founder Nicole Spooner grew up fostering and volunteering with various animal rescue organizations and caring for her own pets. She took her true love of animals and became a Veterinary Technician.

Ten years ago, Nicole turned this passion into a business that could benefit both pets and the owners who love them. She wanted her business to stand out from other dog-walking businesses, making it more personal for both the clients and their furry friends.

Instead of constantly switching up the walkers like other companies, DoggieWalks strives to create long lasting, personal relationships between dogs and their walkers to maximize their self-growth and ensure their individual needs are being met. By assigning each dog the same walker for every walk, we see happier dogs, happier walkers, and happier clients.
