Welcome to Doggie Walks!


Thank you for visiting my Dog Walking/Pet Sitting Website! My name is Nicole Spooner and DoggieWalks is my company that I've created to take my love of working with animals to a place where both pets and people can benefit.

I am a Veterinary Technician with 16 years of animal care experience and training. I love animals, they are my life. Nothing makes me happier than spending time with pets. I love all pets, from Guinea pigs to Great Dane's- it doesn't matter, they are all family to me!

My services include but are not limited to: Dog Walking, Pet Sitting (kitties and doggies), Group Walks, and Dog and Cat Visits while you are away.
Please check out my About Me Page to learn more about my experience and what made me the animal lover that I am today!

Also, check out my rates, I am sure you will not be disappointed as I know they are very reasonable.

NEW CLIENT bonus: if you and your pet like me and recommend me to a friend of yours, I will give you 50% off your next visit!
